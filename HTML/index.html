My Values

  • Family: I value family because they have helped me through everything and it can motivate me to do better every day. No matter what happens, my family will always be there.   • Passion: I value passion because doing something I like gives me satisfaction and allows me to just have fun. It also helps me to relax from stressful times. I can talk about something I'm passionate about for seemingly forever and the quality of the work that I do is great for things I am passionate about because I put alot of heart into it

My Personality

According to 16Personalities, I am a mediator. I feel that I am more of an introvert than an extrovert. I am also very passionate, putting my all into my interests. However, I can be unrealistic, picturing everything perfectly only to result in failure. Another one of my weaknesses is that I can be self critical, which can greatly affect my self-esteem and motivation for future projects. To overcome these challenges, I need to have realistic plan and acknowledge that things may not go the way I want them to.
