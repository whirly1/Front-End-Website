Career Options

Artificial Intelligence (AI) Applications Developer

AI Applications Developers develop the artificial intelligence software and applications that a particular business might use. They program systems that will change to suit the business's needs based on the data collected and analyzed.

Some of the duties of an AI developer is to design, develop and monitor AI systems. They also develop data ingest and data transformation architecture. Some tools a developer might use are programming languages, deep learning platforms, analytic tools and more.

There are many skills needed for AI developers. They need to know about Mathematics and Algorithms, Probability and Statistics, Programming Languages, Flexibility and Quick Learning, and Multi-Tasking and Proficiency With Multiple Computing Tools. These are all skills I will learn during my time in SP.

Data Analyst

A data analyst uses analytical tools and methods to transform sometimes confusing, disorganized or disparate data into information that's readable and easy to understand. They also pinpoint trends and patterns.

Some responsibilities of data analysts are to gather data. Data might come from tracking tools like Google Analytics, company surveys, email marketing software, or any number of sources. They also have to design and maintain databases, clean data, documenting and improving processes, and more.

Some of the skills every data analyst should have are Math and Statisical Skills, Excel Proficiency, Data Visualization, Programming and Database Tools. I will also learn these across the 3 years learning in SP.

Data Scientist

Data scientists are a new breed of analytical data expert who have the technical skills to solve complex problems - and the curiosity to explore what problems need to be solved.

Typical job duties of a data scientist are collecting large amounts of data and transforming them into a more usable format, staying on top of analytical techniques such as machine learning, deep learning and text analytics, having a solid grasp of statistics, including statistical tests and distributions, and more.

Data Scientists have to be familiar with Data Visualization, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Pattern Recognition, Data Preparation and Text Analytics. SP will equip me with these skills throughout my course.
